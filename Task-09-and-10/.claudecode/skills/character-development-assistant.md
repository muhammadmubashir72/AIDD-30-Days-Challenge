# Character Development Assistant

## Skill Name
Character Development Assistant

## Purpose
Helps writers create deep, believable characters with clear motivations, backstories, and personality traits.

## How It Works
Takes character information and fills in details about background, personality, goals, fears, and relationships to make characters more three-dimensional.

## Input Example
```
Character: Main protagonist
Role: Young detective solving supernatural mysteries
Age: 22 years old
Basic trait: Curious and determined
```

## Output Example
```
# Character Profile: Maya Chen

## Basic Information
- Name: Maya Chen
- Age: 22
- Occupation: Private investigator specializing in supernatural cases

## Personality Traits
- Curious and analytical
- Determined and persistent
- Slightly stubborn
- Empathetic but guarded after past trauma

## Backstory
- Lost her grandmother (who taught her about supernatural phenomena) when she was 18
- Felt dismissed by police due to her age and unconventional methods
- Started her own practice after having success with small supernatural cases

## Goals and Motivations
- Prove that supernatural cases deserve proper investigation
- Protect people from dangers others don't believe exist
- Find answers about her grandmother's mysterious death

## Relationships
- Trusts few people due to past disappointments
- Close bond with her grandmother's cat who seems to sense supernatural activity
- Tense relationship with local police department

## Internal Conflicts
- Struggles between wanting recognition and wanting privacy
- Feels burdened by responsibility of helping people others can't
```
