# Chapter Outline Generator

## Skill Name
Chapter Outline Generator

## Purpose
Helps writers create detailed outlines for book chapters, ensuring proper structure and logical flow of ideas.

## How It Works
Takes a general topic or story concept and generates a structured outline with sections, subsections, and key points to cover in each part of the chapter.

## Input Example
```
Topic: Character development in fantasy novels
Target audience: Young adult readers
Key themes: Friendship, courage, magic
```

## Output Example
```
# Chapter Title: The Discovery of Magic Powers

## Section 1: Introduction
- Introduce main character's normal life
- Show hint of something unusual about the protagonist
- Set up the inciting incident

## Section 2: First Magic Experience
- Character accidentally uses magic
- Reaction of fear and confusion
- Confidant reveals truth about character's heritage

## Section 3: Learning and Growth
- Mentor figure introduces magical training
- Challenges character faces while learning
- Strengthening of friendship bonds with fellow students

## Section 4: Consequences and Decisions
- Character realizes the danger of their powers
- Difficult choice between safety and helping others
- Decision to accept responsibility
```
